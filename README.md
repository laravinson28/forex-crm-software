## Core Modules of Our Forex CRM Software

### Admin CRM Panel

The command center for brokerage management. Admins control user roles and permissions, track fund movements, review sales pipelines, approve withdrawals, and monitor compliance — all from one dashboard. Role-based access ensures every team member only sees what they need.

### Back Office Portal

Designed for your internal operations team. The back office handles client account management, transaction processing, document verification, IB monitoring, and support escalations. Real-time sync across the platform means no data lag between departments.

### Trader's Room

The client-facing side of the CRM. Traders use this portal to deposit and withdraw funds, view account statements, track their trade history, upload KYC documents, and communicate with support. A clean, intuitive interface keeps traders confident and reduces churn.

Every user — admin, sales rep, compliance officer, or IB — operates within clearly defined permissions. Sensitive data stays protected while teams work efficiently within their scope.

The **Forex CRM MT4/MT5 integration** bridges your trading servers and CRM in real time. Account balances, margin levels, open positions, and trade history stay synchronized without manual intervention. Vertex is also fully supported.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What makes Forex CRM Software different from a standard CRM?**

A standard CRM manages contacts and pipelines. A Forex CRM goes further — it connects directly to trading servers, automates KYC compliance, manages multi-level IB commissions, and handles regulated payment flows. It is built for the operational reality of running a brokerage.

**Q: Can the Forex CRM integrate with both MT4 and MT5?**

Yes. The platform supports full bi-directional sync with MetaTrader 4, MetaTrader 5, and Vertex. Account data, trade history, and balance updates flow automatically without manual imports.

**Q: Is white label Forex CRM suitable for new brokerages?**

Absolutely. White label is often the preferred choice for new entrants because it eliminates the time and cost of building from scratch. You get a production-ready platform with your branding active from day one.

**Q: How does the IB management module handle commission tiers?**

The IB module supports unlimited hierarchy depth. Commissions are calculated automatically per tier based on rules you configure — per lot, revenue share, or hybrid models. Payouts are tracked and processed with full audit trails.

**Q: What compliance standards does the KYC/AML module support?**

The compliance engine is configurable for FCA, CySEC, ASIC, FSCA, and other major regulatory frameworks. It supports document verification, sanctions screening, risk scoring, and SAR generation.
